Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TarmacSync collects, uses, stores, and protects information submitted through our website, pilot inquiries, product demonstrations, and airport pilot activities.

Effective date: June 25, 2026

1. Who we are

TarmacSync is an airport procurement intelligence pilot built for U.S. airport teams. The service helps airport teams evaluate purchase needs, possible buying routes, missing information, internal decision readiness, and related documentation needs. TarmacSync is not a procurement system of record, legal advisor, grant consultant, or FAA approval authority.

For privacy questions, contact us at contact@tarmacsync.com.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information: name, title, airport or organization, work email, phone number, and inquiry details.
  • Pilot and demo information: purchase scenarios, pilot goals, airport type, general funding context, and feedback you voluntarily provide.
  • Account information: login credentials, user role, preferences, and authentication metadata if accounts are enabled.
  • Airport-provided materials: procurement policies, purchasing thresholds, example purchase requests, contract references, board memo examples, or other files voluntarily shared for pilot configuration or testing.
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referral source, approximate location, timestamps, and diagnostic logs.
  • Communications: emails, meeting notes, support requests, survey responses, and other messages exchanged with TarmacSync.

3. Information we do not intentionally request

During the pilot phase, please do not submit classified information, sensitive security information, law-enforcement-sensitive information, export-controlled material, protected personal information, payment card numbers, social security numbers, health information, or confidential third-party data unless a separate written agreement with TarmacSync specifically authorizes that exchange.

4. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Respond to pilot inquiries and schedule demonstrations.
  • Evaluate whether an airport is a good fit for the pilot.
  • Configure, test, and improve TarmacSync’s purchase-guidance workflow.
  • Provide product support and communicate about the pilot.
  • Analyze website performance, reliability, and product usage.
  • Protect the service from misuse, security threats, and unauthorized access.
  • Maintain business records and comply with applicable legal obligations.

5. AI and product improvement

TarmacSync may use AI-enabled tools to process purchase scenarios, summarize text, identify missing information, and generate draft planning outputs. During the pilot phase, we may also review user feedback and product activity to improve accuracy, clarity, and workflow design.

We do not intend to use airport-provided pilot materials to publicly identify an airport, disclose confidential airport information, or train public-facing marketing materials without permission. Any production use of airport materials for model training, benchmarking, or case studies should be governed by a separate written agreement.

6. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies to understand website traffic, improve performance, remember preferences, and measure pilot interest. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. Some website features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

7. Service providers

We may share information with trusted service providers that help us operate the website, host the product, provide email, manage authentication, process analytics, schedule meetings, store files, monitor errors, or support AI functionality. These providers are authorized to use information only as needed to provide services to TarmacSync, subject to their own terms and privacy commitments.

8. How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers supporting TarmacSync operations.
  • With your permission or at your direction.
  • To comply with applicable law, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
  • To protect TarmacSync, users, airports, or the public from fraud, security threats, or misuse.
  • As part of a business transaction such as merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.

9. Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. No internet-based service is completely secure. Airports should avoid submitting highly sensitive materials unless security requirements have been reviewed and agreed in writing.

10. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, manage the pilot, improve the product, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain business records. Pilot participants may request deletion or return of certain pilot materials, subject to legal, security, backup, and recordkeeping limitations.

11. Public-sector records

Some airport sponsors are public entities subject to public-records or freedom-of-information laws. TarmacSync cannot guarantee that information exchanged with a public airport will be exempt from disclosure under applicable law. Airport users should consult their own counsel before sharing sensitive or confidential records.

12. Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal information. You may also unsubscribe from non-essential communications. We may need to retain limited information for legal, security, contractual, or legitimate business purposes.

13. Children’s privacy

TarmacSync is intended for professional and business use by airport teams. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date and may provide additional notice where appropriate.

15. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to contact@tarmacsync.com.
